Bio

Sarah Thorne is a student at the Nashville Software School. She has a background as a professional creative in many capacities, including writing and music. Her hobbies include absorbing fantasy literature and caring for her precious hedgehog.

She first turned her attention towards web development when she observed that she used spreadsheeting as an emotional pick-me-up. To her delight, her hunch is working out and development is proving and interesting, enjoyable, and pleasantly challenging pursuit.

Her favorite language so far is JavaScript. JavaScript embodies much of what she most loves about development--puzzle-solving, creativity, attention to detail, and instant gratification when the code finally runs.
